What kind of trip can I host?

ANYTHING

The only bounds are your imagination & creativity

In the ever changing world of the Internet, we see the world being more connected than ever. However, this connectivity has come at a cost to us. We have lost the ability to start a conversation with any stranger around us. We, at TripBuddy.sg, are passionate about providing a truly local experience to our fellow exchange students in a way that has never been explored before. Along with this, we also offer our fellow NTU students the opportunity to network with the world without leaving the comfort of Singapore. The best part — you get extra pocket money too!

Think about it like a part-time job (with no bosses or administrative hassles) or an opportunity to promote your CCAs. We believe in lubricating the process of making new friends from around the world in the hopes that people might make lifelong friends in the process.
